# Client Work

This folder contains deliverables produced across 13+ client engagements completed through the [NorCal Small Business Development Center (SBDC)](https://norcalsbdc.org/) between 2025 and 2026.

Each subfolder represents one client engagement. Client names have been anonymized (Client-A through Client-M) to protect confidentiality per SBDC advising guidelines. Folder READMEs describe the industry, engagement scope, and deliverables produced.

Work spanned discovery and audit, strategy development, tool implementation, and execution support across 8 industries including healthcare, food & beverage, education, retail, nonprofit, language services, beauty & wellness, and professional services.
