# Client-G — Healthcare / Chiropractic

**Industry:** Healthcare / Chiropractic
**Engagement:** NorCal SBDC Marketing Advisory

## Overview
Advised a chiropractic practice on building a patient acquisition and retention marketing system. Work spanned SEO and messaging strategy, a three-phase patient outreach framework, a two-platform social media strategy with Buffer scheduling, and retention-focused content targeting key patient drop-off mindsets.

| `Client-G_SocialMedia_Strategy.docx` | Two-platform social media strategy with step-by-step Buffer, Instagram, and TikTok setup |
